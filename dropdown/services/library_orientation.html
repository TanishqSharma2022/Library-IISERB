Library Orientation

Central Library offers the orientation program to the students and others in the beginning of every academic year. Through this program, an effort is made to familiarize every new member with the resources, services, and facilities offered by Central Library and its policies to use the same. Central Library will be happy to design and offer training programs to assist users to develop the ability to locate, identify, use, and interpret information effectively. Central Library would like to promote the role of the library as a resource for lifelong learning, through this program.
